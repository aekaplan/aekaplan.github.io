The Design Process
at 8th Light, Inc.

A simple guide to how we work

↓

Discovery

Before we start the design process, we want to get to know your product and its users.

We will meet with you, brainstorm ideas, and sketch them out together to figure out where to begin.

Discovery takes 2 to 7 days.

Wireframing

We'll help solidify your ideas by creating wireframes for your project. Wireframing is a basic visual guide used to suggest the layout of fundamental elements in a web interface.

This stage allows us to explore all of our options quickly, focusing only on layout and usability, so that we get feedback as early as possible.

The tool we use is called Balsamiq.

Wireframing takes 7 to 14 days.

Prototyping

The next phase in our process is building a static prototype. Based on our wireframes, we'll start structuring things in the browser using HTML and CSS.

This allows the developers on the team to start implementing functionality with the design earlier in the process.

Prototyping takes 7 to 21 days.

User Testing

Light user testing is done throughout the design and development process. It can be done after wireframing, prototyping, or implementation.

We feel that the earlier and more often user testing is done, the better your application will be in the end.

User Testing takes 1 to 2 days (each time).

Visual Design

The visual design process is one of the most exciting steps. It's the time when we begin layering in color, texture and perfect the use of typography.

Because we care about every little detail, we spend a lot of time perfecting and polishing the entire application.

Visual Design takes 2 to 3 weeks.

Implementation

Depending on your project's timeline, we can go from wireframes to implementation. We'll provide feedback, wireframes and go right into implementing the design.

We understand time and budget constraints and are here to help you craft a product that your users will fall in love with.

Implementation takes 3 to 6 weeks.
